Our Story

Where Trust Meets Training

At MObedience Kennels, we believe every dog has the potential to be a wonderful companion — they just need the right guidance. Our approach is built on science-backed techniques, clear communication, and genuine care for both dogs and their families.

We're based in Missouri and proud to serve our local community. Whether your dog is six months or six years old, there's always a path forward. We've helped hundreds of families transform their relationships with their dogs, and we'd love to help yours.

Every dog is different, which is why we customize our programs to fit your dog's unique temperament and your family's lifestyle. No cookie-cutter approaches here — just real, lasting results.
